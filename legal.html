Privacy & Data Policy

Learn how Shopora collects, stores, uses, and protects your personal information.

›

Shopora values user privacy and is committed to maintaining transparent data practices. We collect only the information necessary to provide shopping services, customer support, fraud prevention, and order fulfillment.

  • • User account information includes name, email address, shipping address, and order history.
  • • Payment details are processed securely through PCI-compliant third-party gateways.
  • • Users may request data export, modification, or deletion at any time.
  • • Analytics and cookies are used to improve site performance and personalization.
  • • Shopora never sells personal customer data to unauthorized third parties.
